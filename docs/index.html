Our Mission

Summit Street Technologies exists to give clients of every size access to principled, experienced technology guidance. Technology is most valuable when it serves a clear business purpose — and that belief drives every engagement. Every client receives the depth of a specialist and the breadth of a generalist, applied to the decisions, strategies, and software that move their business forward. Rooted in Napa and Sonoma and serving the Bay Area and select clients nationwide, Summit Street Technologies is built on partnership, precision, and a genuine commitment to your success.

His background spans every layer of the stack and every stage of the software lifecycle, from defining technical vision as an enterprise architect to writing and shipping production code as a principal engineer, bringing both the strategic perspective of a leader and the technical credibility of a practitioner to every engagement.
